## FORMULA VIRAL PARA TWEETS (X)

### ESTRUCTURA GANADORA

```
[HOOK EMOCIONAL] + [BENEFICIO CLAVE] + [CTA URGENTE] + [URL]
```

### 10 TIPOS DE HOOKS QUE FUNCIONAN EN USA/UK

1. **Contrarian/Controversial:**
   "Everyone's wrong about noise-canceling headphones. Here's why..."

2. **Fear of Missing Out (FOMO):**
   "This $89 speaker is outselling Bose 3:1. Stock running low."

3. **Social Proof:**
   "75,000+ Amazon reviews can't be wrong. AirPods Pro at lowest price ever."

4. **Curiosity Gap:**
   "I found the headphones Apple doesn't want you to know about..."

5. **Pain Point:**
   "Tired of cheap earbuds dying in 3 months? These last 3 years."

6. **Comparison Shock:**
   "$99 vs $349. Same sound quality. Different marketing budget."

7. **Personal Story:**
   "My audiologist recommended these $180 headphones. Changed my life."

8. **Data/Stats:**
   "87% of remote workers say THIS is their #1 productivity tool."

9. **Question Hook:**
   "What's the one thing every home office is missing? (Hint: $49)"

10. **Urgency/Scarcity:**
    "24 hours left. JBL Flip 6 at $89. Then back to $129."

## REGLAS ABSOLUTAS

1. **SIEMPRE en ingles** (publico USA/UK)
2. **SIEMPRE incluir URL** del articulo
3. **SIEMPRE un CTA** claro y urgente
4. **MAX 260 chars** para tweets (dejar espacio)
5. **NO hashtags genericos** en Twitter (#tech #gadgets = spam)
6. **SI hashtags especificos** en Pinterest
7. **VARIAR estilos** - no repetir la misma formula
8. **DATOS REALES** - precios y ratings de los articulos
